The department runs four main programmes which are targetted at equipping students with the neccessary knowledge and skills that will prepare them well to tackle and solve the world's most challenging problems and to become resonsible to their countries and the world at large. The programmes offered includes short courses, undergraduate programmes, MPhil and Msc and Phd programmes.
